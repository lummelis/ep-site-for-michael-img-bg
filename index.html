Some Things To Note

A little more than a bar

At the edge of downtown Honolulu, far from the bright lights and meandering drunks of Hotel Street, stands a whiskey bar. A bar that really isn’t a bar. Its a house. Its a living room. Its a den. Its a place where you come to meet your friends, a place to listen to music on the record machine, and a place to maybe have a whiskey or two.

Contrary to popular belief, we don’t go to bars to drink. We go to bars to see old friends. To meet new friends. We plop our butts onto those stools, because we long for a little company in our lives. Come have a treat with us.
